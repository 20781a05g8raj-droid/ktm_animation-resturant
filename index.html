Our Story

From the streets of Raxaul, with love

The Beginning

It all started with a simple dream — to bring the authentic, soul-warming flavours of Nepali-Indian momos to the heart of Bihar. In a small corner of Raxaul, with a stove, a passion for food, and recipes passed down through generations, KTM Momos was born.

Our Philosophy

We believe that every momo tells a story. Fresh ingredients sourced daily, traditional recipes refined over years, and a modern presentation that celebrates the humble dumpling. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just pure love, folded into every single piece.

The Journey

From a small stall to a beloved restaurant with 166+ 5-star reviews, the love from our community keeps us going. Every customer who walks through our doors becomes family. And every plate that leaves our kitchen carries a little piece of Raxaul's heart with it.

Our Journey

2020

Humble beginnings — KTM Momos opens its doors in Raxaul with just two signature items on the menu.

2021

First 100 happy customers. Word-of-mouth spreads like wildfire across Raxaul.

2023

Menu expanded to include Jhol Momos, Chicken Wings, Paneer Pakora & more. Delivery partnerships with Zomato go live.

2024

4.8 ⭐ rating achieved. Crossed 166 verified reviews. Officially the most-loved momo spot in the region.

Today

Still growing. Still folding every momo by hand. Still serving Raxaul with the same love and fire.
